About Perri7

Building brands that inspire and endure

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Perri7 has grown from a boutique licensing agency to a leading force in fashion and homegoods brand licensing. Our journey began with a simple belief: great brands deserve great partners who understand their vision and can translate it into products that consumers love.

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between brands and consumers through exceptional licensed products that enhance everyday life. We strive to be the licensing partner of choice by delivering innovative solutions, superior quality, and measurable results.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in fashion and homegoods licensing, recognized for our creativity, integrity, and ability to transform brands into lifestyle experiences that resonate across generations and cultures.

Our Values

Integrity

We build lasting relationships through honest communication, transparency, and ethical business practices in everything we do.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and stay ahead of trends to deliver fresh, relevant products that capture consumer imagination.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of quality in product design, manufacturing, and customer service.

Partnership

We view our brand relationships as true partnerships, working collaboratively to achieve shared success.

Sustainability

We prioritize environmentally responsible practices and work to minimize our impact on the planet.

Diversity

We celebrate different perspectives and believe diverse teams create better products and stronger brands.

Our Expertise

Fashion & Apparel

With deep roots in the fashion industry, we understand the nuances of apparel design, production, and retail. Our team has successfully launched collections spanning casual wear, activewear, outerwear, accessories, and luxury fashion.

  • Men's, Women's, and Children's apparel
  • Footwear and accessories
  • Active and performance wear
  • Luxury and premium collections

Homegoods & Lifestyle

We bring brands into the home through thoughtfully designed products that enhance living spaces. Our homegoods expertise covers everything from textiles to furniture, creating cohesive lifestyle collections.

  • Bedding and bath products
  • Home decor and accessories
  • Furniture and lighting
  • Kitchen and dining items

Leadership Team

Our experienced leadership team brings decades of combined expertise in licensing, retail, and brand management.
