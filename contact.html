Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my brand for licensing consideration?

Please fill out the contact form above, selecting "Licensing Opportunities" as your inquiry type. Include information about your brand, target market, and licensing goals. Our team will review your submission and respond within 5-7 business days.

What is your typical licensing agreement structure?

Every licensing agreement is customized to fit the specific needs of the brand and product category. We typically work on a royalty-based model with minimum guarantees. Contact our licensing team to discuss details specific to your brand.

Do you work with emerging brands or only established names?

We partner with brands at various stages of development. While brand recognition is important, we also value innovation, strong design, and clear market positioning. We evaluate each opportunity on its individual merits.

How long does it take to bring a licensed product to market?

The timeline varies depending on product category and complexity, but typically ranges from 9-18 months from agreement to retail availability. This includes design, development, manufacturing, and distribution phases.

What markets do you operate in?

We have global capabilities with licensed products available in over 40 countries. Our strongest markets are North America, Europe, and Asia, with growing presence in emerging markets.

Are you currently hiring?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Please send your resume and cover letter to license@perri7.com, and visit our About page to learn more about our company culture and values.
