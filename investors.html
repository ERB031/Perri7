Our Business Model

Revenue Streams

Licensing Royalties

Ongoing royalty payments based on wholesale or retail sales of licensed products, typically ranging from 5-15% depending on category and brand.

Minimum Guarantees

Contractual minimum annual payments from licensees, providing predictable baseline revenue and protecting against market volatility.

Upfront Fees

Initial licensing fees for new partnerships and brand launches, supporting development costs and providing immediate returns.

Strategic Services

Consulting, design, and brand strategy services provided to partners, generating additional fee-based revenue.

Competitive Advantages

  • Diversified portfolio across fashion and homegoods reduces category risk
  • Long-term contracts (typically 3-7 years) provide revenue stability
  • Established relationships with major retailers ensure distribution
  • Experienced management team with deep industry expertise
  • Scalable business model with high margin potential
  • Strong brand partners with loyal consumer followings

Growth Strategy

Expand Brand Portfolio

Actively pursuing new licensing agreements with emerging and established brands across complementary categories. Target: 5-8 new brands annually.

Geographic Expansion

Growing presence in Asia-Pacific and Latin American markets while deepening penetration in existing North American and European territories.

Category Extension

Expanding existing brand partnerships into adjacent product categories to maximize revenue per license.

Direct-to-Consumer

Developing e-commerce capabilities and owned-retail concepts to capture higher margins and consumer data.

Sustainability Leadership

Investing in sustainable materials and practices to meet consumer demand and differentiate our offerings.

Strategic Acquisitions

Evaluating complementary licensing agencies and brand assets to accelerate growth and add capabilities.
